Utilities Distribution

  • Full Stack
  • Databases

    • My team members Yadhap Dahal, Thomas Gracie, Sheldon Greiling, and I, embarked on a full stack development project during CS6400 in the Summer of 2023.     Youtube Content

  • Regression
  • Network Graph
  • NLP

    • The project explores YouTube trending videos to understand factors affecting video popularity and cross-cultural preferences. By leveraging NLP and regression modeling our team was able to analyze video data from major English-speaking countries. This led us to develop 3 different tools aimed to enhance content creators' reach and adaptability to cultural variances in video engagement. The first tool was a predcition of trendiness based on likes, dislikes, and video categories. The second tool was a Suggestive Title Maker using LSTM models to generate engaging titles. The third tool was a Tagging Network Recommendation system for content discoverability across english speaking regions.
